About Me

My name is Reed Wilson, I graduated from Richard O Jacobson technical high school. I enjoy playing videogames, going to the gym, and playing soccer. I have competed in the Connect-it NGT 360 competition and won 3rd place. I worked with two teammates to create an app that allows users to budget and save while grocery shopping. NGT 360 gave me an abundance of experience in real world business and programming through connections, mentors, tasks, and attending the Synapse Summit. In addition to competing in NGT 360, I have programed games in unity throughout high school. While in high school, I was able to play soccer and win the Figo cup in Lakeland with Celtic SC. After playing for Celtic, I moved to the Clearwater chargers where I played for the rest of my soccer experience.
